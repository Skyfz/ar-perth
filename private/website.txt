## 3. Page: The Model (Build-to-Rent & Impact)

*Use this section to educate stakeholders based on the "Build-to-rent" article provided.*

### **What is Build-to-Rent (BTR)?**
Unlike the traditional "build-to-sell" model where apartments are sold to individual investors, AR Perth retains ownership of the development. This institutional ownership model allows us to:
1.  **Control Quality:** We build durable, high-quality assets because we are responsible for their long-term maintenance.
2.  **Create Community:** We curate amenities that foster connection—co-working spaces, communal gardens, and shared fleets.
3.  **Stabilize Supply:** We deliver housing at scale (hundreds of units per site) rather than drip-feeding the market.

### **Impact Housing: Support for Key Workers**
Affordable housing is no longer solely a poverty issue; it is a mainstream economic necessity.
* **Our Focus:** We dedicate a significant percentage of our portfolio to "Key Worker Housing." This ensures that the people who keep Perth running—hospital staff, emergency responders, teachers—can afford to live near where they work.
* **Social & Disability:** We are actively exploring partnerships to include Specialist Disability Accommodation (SDA) within our mixed-use precincts, promoting inclusivity.

## 4. Page: Construction Innovation (The "How")

*This section leverages the user's background in tech/automation and addresses the "productivity" issues raised in Cathryn Carver’s speech.*

### **Overcoming the "Speed Limit" of Construction**
Productivity Commission research highlights that traditional builds take 60% longer than they did a decade ago. Regulatory red tape, labor shortages, and material costs are stalling the industry.

**AR Perth** addresses this through **Advanced Construction Methodology:**
* **Modular & Prefabrication:** By moving construction elements off-site, we reduce weather delays and reliance on on-site labor availability. This allows us to deliver projects in 18 months, not 28.
* **Tech-First Planning:** We utilize advanced digital twins and project management automation to navigate planning approvals and zoning complexities efficiently.
* **Supply Chain Resilience:** Leveraging Alleyroads' global procurement network, we mitigate the material cost blowouts that have caused insolvency for over 2,300 construction companies in the last year.
